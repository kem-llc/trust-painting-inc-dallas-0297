Cabinet Painting

Give your cabinets a fresh, modern look.

Schedule this service

Overview

Transform your kitchen or bathroom with cabinet painting. We use specialized techniques and durable paints to achieve a factory-like finish without the cost of replacement.

What's included

  • Cost-effective alternative to replacement
  • Custom color options to match your style
  • Quick turnaround with minimal downtime

How it works

1

Removal & Cleaning

We remove doors and hardware, then clean all surfaces.

2

Sanding & Priming

We sand and apply a high-adhesion primer.

3

Spraying & Reassembly

We spray paint for a smooth finish, then reinstall doors and hardware.
