Cabinet & Furniture Painting

Give your cabinets and furniture a fresh, modern look with our specialty painting.

Schedule this service

Overview

Revitalize your kitchen, bathroom, or office with our cabinet and furniture painting service. We use high-quality paints and techniques to achieve a smooth, factory-like finish. It's a cost-effective alternative to replacement.

What's included

  • Cost-effective makeover
  • Custom colors and finishes
  • Durable, scratch-resistant surface
  • Quick turnaround time

How it works

1

Removal & Cleaning

We remove doors and hardware, then thoroughly clean and degrease all surfaces.

2

Sanding & Priming

We sand and apply a bonding primer to ensure the paint adheres properly.

3

Spraying & Reassembly

We spray multiple thin coats for a smooth finish, then reinstall hardware and doors.
