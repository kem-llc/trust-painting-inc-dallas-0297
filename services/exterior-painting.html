Overview

Our exterior painting service includes thorough surface preparation, pressure washing, and application of weather-resistant paints. We enhance curb appeal and protect against the elements.

What's included

  • Boosts curb appeal and property value
  • Protects against sun, rain, and wind
  • Long-lasting color and finish

How it works

1

Inspection & Prep

We assess surfaces, power wash, and repair any damage.

2

Priming & Caulking

We prime bare wood and seal gaps for a smooth base.

3

Painting

We apply two coats of premium exterior paint for even coverage.
