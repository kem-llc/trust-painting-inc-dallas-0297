Our story

Dallas's Trusted Painting Experts Since 2008

Our mission is to deliver exceptional painting services that exceed expectations, using premium materials and skilled craftsmanship, while maintaining integrity and respect for every client's home or business.

Trust Painting Inc. was founded in 2008 by a team of experienced painters who saw a need for reliable, high-quality painting services in Dallas. Starting with small residential projects, we built a reputation for meticulous attention to detail and outstanding customer service. Over the years, we've expanded to include commercial and specialty painting, serving clients across the Dallas metroplex.

Today, we are a family-owned business that treats every project as if it were our own home. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay updated on the latest techniques and products. We take pride in our work and are committed to making your painting experience stress-free and rewarding.
